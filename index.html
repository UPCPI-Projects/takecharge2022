    BIBLE
    TRIVIA
    • 1 PARTICIPANT PER LOCAL CHURCH.
    • THE PARTICIPANT MUST BE ABLE TO STATE/RECITE THE 66 BOOKS OF THE BIBLE, FROM GENESIS TO REVELATION, THEN REVELATION TO GENESIS.
    • THE TIMER WILL ADD 1 SECOND IN EVERY UNCLEAR PRONUNCIATION, SKIPPED, AND REPEATED BOOKS UPON RECITING.
    • IT IS NOT ALLOWED TO DO SHORTCUTS LIKE (EXAMPLE, | AND II SAMUEL. THE PARTICIPANT MUST MENTION I SAMUEL, II SAMUEL.
    • PRONUNCIATION MUST BE CLEAR, FACILITATORS WILL RECORD THE ENTIRE RECITATION, AND TURN THE AUDIO INTO A SLOW MOTION, OR CHANGE THE PLAYBACK SPEED INTO 0.5 FOR CLEARER HEARING.
    • THE FACILITATORS WILL LISTEN TO THE AUDIO RECORDED RECITATION OF THE 66 BOOKS COUNTDOWN AND COUNT-UP FOR CHECKING
    • THE THREE CHURCHES, FASTEST TO FINISH THE ARRANGEMENT BASED ON THEIR TIME, WILL BE AWARDED AS THE FIRST, SECOND, AND THIRD
    BIBLE
    MEMORY VERSE
    • 2 PARTICIPANTS PER LOCAL CHURCH.
    • PARTICIPANTS MUST BRING/PROVIDE THEIR HARDCOPY OF THEIR VERSES.
    • VERSES CAN BE EITHER DOCTRINAL OR INSPIRATIONAL.
    • THE 2 PARTICIPANTS CAN RECITE VERSES AS MUCH AS THEY CAN IN 3 MINUTES.
    • VERSES ARE INVALID/NOT COUNTED IF ONE WORD IS MISSING OR ADDED.
    • PRONUNCIATION MUST BE CLEAR, FACILITATORS WILL RECORD THE ENTIRE RECITAL, AND TURN THE AUDIO INTO A SLOW MOTION OR SET THE PLAYBACK SPEED TO. 0.5 FOR CLEARER HEARING.
    • THE THREE CHURCHES WITH THE MOST VERSES TO MEMORIZE REGARDING WILL BE AWARDED AS FIRST, SECOND, AND THIRD.
    BIBLE
    COUNTDOWN/UP
    • 1 PARTICIPANT PER LOCAL CHURCH.
    • THE PARTICIPANT MUST BE ABLE TO STATE/RECITE THE 66 BOOKS OF THE BIBLE, FROM GENESIS TO REVELATION, THEN REVELATION TO GENESIS.
    • THE TIMER WILL ADD 1 SECOND IN EVERY UNCLEAR PRONUNCIATION, SKIPPED, AND REPEATED BOOKS UPON RECITING.
    • IT IS NOT ALLOWED TO DO SHORTCUTS LIKE (EXAMPLE, | AND II SAMUEL. THE PARTICIPANT MUST MENTION I SAMUEL, II SAMUEL.
    • PRONUNCIATION MUST BE CLEAR, FACILITATORS WILL RECORD THE ENTIRE RECITATION, AND TURN THE AUDIO INTO A SLOW MOTION, OR CHANGE THE PLAYBACK SPEED INTO 0.5 FOR CLEARER HEARING.
    • THE FACILITATORS WILL LISTEN TO THE AUDIO RECORDED RECITATION OF THE 66 BOOKS COUNTDOWN AND COUNT-UP FOR CHECKING
    • THE THREE CHURCHES, FASTEST TO FINISH THE ARRANGEMENT BASED ON THEIR TIME, WILL BE AWARDED AS THE FIRST, SECOND, AND THIRD
    BIBLE
    MATH
    • 2 PARTICIPANTS PER LOCAL CHURCH.
    • PARTICIPANTS WILL BE GIVEN AN ILLUSTRATION BOARD AND A CHALK FOR SOLVING AND SHOWING THEIR ANSWERS.
    • BIBLE MATH HAS THREE ROUNDS :
      1. EASY (2 POINTS)
      2. AVERAGE (3 POINTS)
      3. DIFFICULT (5 POINTS)
    • TIME FOR EACH ROUND FOR SOLVING :
      1. EASY (10 SECONDS)
      2. AVERAGE (20 SECONDS)
      3. DIFFICULT (30 SECONDS)
    • TYPE OF QUESTIONS FOR EACH ROUND :
      1. EASY (MULTIPLE CHOICE QUESTION)
      2. AVERAGE (MULTIPLE CHOICE QUESTION)
      3. DIFFICULT (IDENTIFICATION - NO CHOICES)
    • PASSING SCORE TO PROCEED TO THE NEXT ROUND :
      1. EASY ROUND(10 QUESTIONS) - PASSING SCORE 10 POINTS AND ABOVE CAN PROCEED TO THE AVERAGE ROUND.
      2. AVERAGE ROUND(10 QUESTIONS) - PASSING SCORE 20 POINTS AND ABOVE CAN PROCEED TO THE DIFFICULT ROUND.
      3. DIFFICULT AND FINAL ROUND(5 QUESTIONS)
    • PARTICIPANTS MUST LIFT THEIR BOARDS AFTER THE TIME, AND WHEN THE FACILITATOR ASKS THEM TO RAISE THEIR ANSWERS.
    • THE FACILITATOR PREPARED 3 TIE BREAKING QUESTIONS
    • SAMPLE QUESTION :
      1. HOW MANY BOOKS ARE THERE IN THE OLD TESTAMENT + BOOKS IN THE NEW TESTAMENT = 66
    • THE THREE CHURCHES WITH THE HIGHEST COMBINED SCORE OF EASY, AVERAGE, AND DIFFICULT ROUNDS WILL BE AWARDED AS THE FIRST, SECOND, AND THIRD.
    BIBLE
    DRILL
    • THE BIBLE DRILL COMPRISES 20 QUESTIONS.
    • 2 PARTICIPANTS PER LOCAL CHURCH.
    • THE FACILITATOR WILL GIVE THE BOOK, CHAPTER, AND THE VERSE (SHUFFLED OR NOT).
    • PARTICIPANTS SHOULD RAISE THEIR BIBLE. PARTICIPANTS SHOULD LISTEN. WHILE THE FACILITATOR IS GIVING THE BOOK, CHAPTER, VERSE (SHUFFLED OR NOT), ONLY TWICE.
    • NOTE THAT THE FACILITATOR WILL SHUFFLE THE TEXT (EXAMPLE, HE/SHE CAN SAY VERSE, CHAPTER, BOOK OR CHAPTER, VERSE, BOOK, ETC.)
    • WAIT FOR THE GO SIGNAL OF THE FACILITATOR BEFORE THE PARTICIPANTS CAN DRILL THE TEXT.
    • THE FACILITATOR WILL ACKNOWLEDGE THE FIRST ONE TO READ THE TEXT, NOT THE ONE WHO STAND FIRST.
    • THERE ARE ASSIGNED PERSONS TO CHECK IF THE PARTICIPANT REALLY DRILLED THE GIVEN TEXT AND DID NOT RECITE IT.
    • THE FIRST PARTICIPANT WHO DRILLED AND STOOD TO READ THE TEXT SHOULD NOT STOP READING THE TEXT UNTIL THE FACILITATOR ACKNOWLEDGES HER/HIS DRILL.
    • THE THREE CHURCHES WHO GOT THE HIGHEST SCORE WILL BE AWARDED AS FIRST, SECOND, AND THIRD.
    BIBLE
    TARGET
    • THE BIBLE TARGET COMPRISES 20 QUESTIONS.
    • 2 PARTICIPANTS PER LOCAL CHURCH.
    • THE FACILITATOR WILL GIVE THE BOOK.
    • THE FACILITATOR WILL GIVE THE BOOK ONLY TWICE. PARTICIPANTS SHOULD LISTEN. WHILE THE FACILITATOR GIVES THE BOOK, PARTICIPANTS SHOULD RAISE THEIR BIBLE.
    • WAIT FOR THE GO SIGNAL OF THE FACILITATOR BEFORE THE PARTICIPANTS TARGET THE BOOK.
    • THE PARTICIPANTS MUST OPEN THE BOOK, AND THE PARTICIPANT WHO TARGETED AND SHOUTED THE NEAREST CHAPTER TO ONE WILL BE GIVEN A POINT. THERE ARE ASSIGNED PERSONS TO CHECK IF THE PARTICIPANT TARGETED THE BOOK AND THE CHAPTER THAT HE/SHE SHOUTED.
    • THE FACILITATOR WILL COUNT 5 SECONDS AFTER GIVING THE BOOK, AND IF,NO ONE TARGETED THE BOOK, THE FACILITATOR WILL PROCEED TO THE NEXT QUESTION.
    • THE PARTICIPANTS MUST SAY THE CHAPTER LOUD IN ORDER FOR THE FACILITATORS TO RECOGNIZE THE PARTICIPANT'S TARGETED BOOK.
    • THE THREE CHURCHES WHO GOT THE HIGHEST SCORE WILL BE AWARDED AS FIRST, SECOND, AND THIRD.
    BIBLE
    HUNT
    • THE BIBLE HUNTING COMPRISES 20 QUESTIONS.
    • 2 PARTICIPANTS PER LOCAL CHURCH.
    • THE FACILITATOR WILL GIVE THE BOOK, THE CHAPTER, THE PHRASE, AND THE TYPE OF PHRASE (SHUFFLED OR NOT).
    • TYPES OF PHRASES ARE:
      1. FIRST PHRASE - BEGINNING OF THE VERSE
      2. MIDDLE PHRASE - MIDDLE OF THE VERSE
      3. LAST PHRASE - LAST PHRASE OF THE VERSE
    • WHILE THE FACILITATOR IS GIVING THE BOOK, CHAPTER, PHRASE, AND THE TYPE OF PHRASE (SHUFFLED OR NOT) ONLY TWICE. PARTICIPANTS SHOULD RAISE THEIR BIBLE. PARTICIPANTS SHOULD LISTEN.
    • WAIT FOR THE GO SIGNAL OF THE FACILITATOR BEFORE THE PARTICIPANTS HUNT THE VERSE.
    • FACILITATOR WILL ACKNOWLEDGE THE FIRST ONE TO SHOUT THE VERSE, NOT THE ONE WHO STANDS FIRST.
    • THE FIRST PARTICIPANT WHO HUNTED AND STOOD TO TELL THE VERSE SHOULD WAIT FOR THE FACILITATOR TO ACKNOWLEDGE HER/HIS HUNT.
    • THE THREE CHURCHES WHO GOT THE HIGHEST SCORE WILL BE AWARDED AS FIRST, SECOND, AND THIRD.
    BIBLE
    ARRANGEMENT
    • 2 PARTICIPANTS PER LOCAL CHURCH.
    • THERE ARE 66 POPSICLE STICKS WHICH COMPRISE THE BOOKS OF THE BIBLE. THE PARTICIPANTS SHOULD ARRANGE ONE LINE FOR THE OLD, ONE LINE FOR THE NEW.
    • THERE WILL BE A TIMER FOR EVERY CHURCH PARTICIPANT.
    • THE SCORER WILL ADD 1 SECOND TO EVERY DISORDERED/DISARRANGED BOOKS (IN ORDER).
    • THE SCORER WILL ALSO ADD 1 SECOND TO EVERY (TANDAY) OF POPSICLES.
    • IF THE PARTICIPANTS ARE DONE ARRANGING, THEY MUST SHOUT "TAKE CHARGE".
    • THEN THE TIMER WILL STOP THE STOPWATCH.
    • FACILITATORS WILL CHECK THE ARRANGEMENT.
    • THE THREE CHURCHES EARLIEST TO FINISH THE ARRANGEMENT BASED ON THEIR TIME WILL BE AWARDED AS THE FIRST, SECOND, AND THIRD.
